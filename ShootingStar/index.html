Shooting Star
  • Shooting star indicates the price could start falling.
  • The formation is bearish because the price tried to rise significantly, sellers took over and pushed the price back down toward the open.
  • Wait to see what the next candle does following a shooting star. If price declines on next candle you may want to sell or short.
  • Price rises after a shooting star, may have been a false signal or the candle is marking a potential resistance area around the price range.
