Technical Indicator

  • Indicators show calculations based on the price and volume.
  • Used to asset entry and exit points for trades.
  • Used for Confirmation!
    • Keep it simple!
    • Use only selective indicators
    • Use indicators that make sense to your Strategy!

Most common Indicators use in Crypto:

  • On-Balance Volume (OBV)
  • Exponential Exponential Moving (EMA)
  • MACD Oscillator (MACD)
  • Stochastic Oscillator (STOCH)
  • Realative Strength Index (RSI)
  • Bollinger Bands
  • Volume (VOL)
