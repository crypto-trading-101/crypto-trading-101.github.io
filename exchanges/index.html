Exchange

You will need Centralized Exchange(CEX) that connect to your bank. You will need to setup up account with one of following.

  • coinbase - I prefer, it’s fast for deposits/withdrawals.
  • crypto.com - I do not have account. Cant’s help you with this exchange.
  • binance.us - Cheap trade fees. Good for Spot Trading. Best for noobs!

Decentralized Dxchange(DEX) for Leverage Trading in USA. Use e-mail option when you register! Do not use phone number!

  • bingx - Offer fake $ for practice and Low fees!
  • primexbt - Nice Bonus and Low fees!
  • phemex - Good for Spot Trading Offers free fees with membership.

I prefer LTF Trading with leverage. I use coinbase to buy Bitcoin and send to DEX exchange. To withdrawal I’ll send Bitcoin from DEX exchange to coinbase.
